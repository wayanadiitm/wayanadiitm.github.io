Wayanad House Archival Repository

Dive into the digital archive of the 3rd & 4th tenures (2023–24 and 2024–25) of the Wayanad House council, IITM BS Program, now preserved on GitHub. This repository is a transparent record of everything that shaped the student life during these two dynamic years — from council meetings and competitions to offline meetups, creative projects and everyday moments. It's a curated reflection of the people, initiatives and spirit that defined the house.
